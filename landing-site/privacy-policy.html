Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 12, 2026

Welcome to AOF Biz. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy describes how AOF Biz handles your personal information when you use our desktop application and associated services.

1. Data We Do NOT Collect

AOF Biz is designed with a "Local First" philosophy. Your business data (orders, inventory, expenses, quotations) is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit your sensitive business data to our servers.

2. Google Drive Integration

AOF Biz offers an optional Cloud Backup feature. To enable this, we request access to your Google Drive via the following scope:

https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.file
Permission requested: "See, edit, create, and delete only the specific Google Drive files you use with this app."

How we use this data:

  • Backup: We create a encrypted backup file of your local database on your Google Drive.
  • Restore: We read the backup files created by AOF Biz to restore your data upon your request.
  • Isolation: We cannot see or access any other files in your Google Drive. We only have access to files created by the AOF Biz application.

3. Licensing & Identity

When you sign in with Google, we collect your Email address. This is used solely for:

  • Verifying your subscription status (Pro/Trial).
  • Providing customer support relative to your account.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Supabase as our identity proxy and licensing server. Supabase handles the authentication securely and does not have access to your private business data stored in your Google Drive.
