Safety Information

As AllSet is currently in its growth phase, browsers or OS might flag the download as "Potentially Unsafe". The app is completely safe to use. Please follow the guides below to bypass these flags.

Windows Guide

  • 1
    Download the .exe installer.
  • 2
    If Chrome warns, select "Keep".
  • 3
    Click "More Info" & "Run anyway" on SmartScreen.

Android Guide

  • 1
    Download the .apk file.
  • 2
    Enable "Unknown Sources" in Settings.
  • 3
    Return and select "Install".
